Celestial Talisman

The Celestial Talisman is a legendary accessory that subtly increases energy regeneration and provides a minor defense boost. Favored by explorers who value sustain in extended fights.

Overview

  • Category: Accessories
  • Type: Amulet
  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Location/How to obtain: Dropped by the Sky Guardian (Third Sea) at a 5% rate or crafted via the Starforge using rare sky materials.

Notes

Best-in-slot for sustain-focused grinding builds.

Legendary rarity makes it valuable in trades.
