Combat — Fighting Style

Combat is the default fighting style received when joining the game. It focuses on basic melee attacks and utility, with fewer moves than other styles.

Contents

  • Moveset
  • Obtainment
  • Notes
  • Trivia

Moveset

MoveKeyDescription
StrikeZBasic melee attack
KickXQuick follow-up attack

Obtainment

Received automatically when starting the game. Cannot be relearned after switching to another style.

Notes

  • Combat has fewer moves than other styles.
  • Does not have destructible physics.

Trivia

  • Combat is the only fighting style received automatically.
