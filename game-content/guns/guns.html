Guns Overview

Guns are weapon classifications of backing-and-zoning, ranged weapons that can be found and bought everywhere in Blox Fruits. Like how guns work in real life or in other games, they will need to be reloaded when shot (which is done automatically), with the exception of their moves. In PvP, guns can be used to provide the player with a strong means of support.

All guns can also be upgraded via the Blacksmith and enchanted via the Dragon Talon Sage to deal extra damage and to gain unique abilities relative to the enchantments.

Explosive Guns

Explosive guns have Area of Effect [M1] attacks that deal slight knockback, can hit multiple enemies and can damage enemies even if the shot does not directly hit. These [M1] attacks have Destructible Physics and are among the few [M1] attacks that can damage Sea Events, making them very useful indeed for Gun Mains. These [M1] attacks have more range than most other guns, but the projectiles are slower.

There are only 3 such guns in the game as of now:

  • Cannon
  • Bazooka
  • Skull Guitar

Notes

  • Guns cannot deal any type of damage to Rubber users, even with Aura.
  • All guns come with two preset skills.
  • Guns' moves ignore reloading, although they have cooldowns, just like any other moves.
  • Some guns may have different ranges and reload speed.
  • Some guns' skills have Destructible Physics.
  • All guns' [M1] attacks cannot be used on Chromebook, unless it has a touchscreen.
  • If the player is playing in console, guns will show in the player's hotbar by name instead of icon.

Trivia

  • Guns can have many different types of projectile weapons, such as:
  • The Venom Bow being a bow
  • The Skull Guitar being a guitar
  • The Refined Slingshot, the Kabucha and the Slingshot being slingshots
