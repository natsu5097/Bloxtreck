Fruits Overview

Blox Fruits are special items that grant users unique abilities and powers. Each fruit has its own strengths, weaknesses, and special moves.

These fruits are scattered throughout the world and can be obtained through various means including purchasing from dealers, defeating bosses, or finding them in the wild.

Blox Fruits Overview

Blox Fruits are mysterious fruits that grant their users supernatural abilities when consumed. As the core mechanic of the game, each fruit provides unique powers and combat advantages. However, these powers come with a price - fruit users will take continuous damage when submerged in water. Some fruits offer special abilities like elemental immunities, enhanced movement, or defensive capabilities.

Fruit Categories

  • Natural: Elemental fruits like Flame, Ice, and Sand
  • Beast: Animal-based fruits like Eagle, Phoenix, and T-Rex
  • Mythical: Legendary fruits with extraordinary powers
  • Awakened: Enhanced versions with unlocked potential

Obtainment

There are many ways to obtain Blox Fruits, with the main ones being:

  • Buying a fruit's powers from the Blox Fruit Dealer.
  • Buying a physical fruit from the Blox Fruit Gacha.
  • Buying a Permanent Blox Fruit from the Blox Fruit Dealer or the in-game shop.
  • Picking up fruits that spawn hourly, or every 45 minutes on weekends.

Other ways include:

  • Doing the most damage in a Factory raid.
  • Defending the Castle on the Sea and dealing the final blow.
  • Defeating a Grand Brigade during Ship or Haunted Ship Raid.
  • Trading fruits at the Café or Mansion.
  • Being gifted a Permanent Blox Fruit.
  • Purchasing a Physical Rocket Fruit from Dog House (Wenlock) for R$ 97.
  • Finding or taking a dropped Blox Fruit.
  • Participating in limited in-game events.
  • Defeating bosses like the Tyrant of the Skies for rare fruit drops.

Notes

  • Dropped fruits do not despawn.
  • Stored fruits cannot be dropped.
  • Permanent fruits can be equipped from the Dealer or Shop; non-permanent fruits cannot.
  • Fruits can be removed, but not recommended.
  • Permanent fruits can be traded if stored in inventory.
  • Mastery and awakened moves persist even after replacement.
  • Sand users take double water damage; Shark membership reduces it; V2 nullifies it.
  • Second and Third Sea fruits can be exchanged for items/events/quests.
  • Buying a fruit with Money $ replaces currently equipped fruit.
  • Some fruits like Ice and Magma v2 allow walking on water but do not fully negate damage.

Trivia

  • It would cost R$ 68,880 to buy every permanent fruit.
  • It would cost $ 84,432,500 to buy every fruit from the Dealer.
  • Most bosses use older versions of their fruits.
  • Don Swan is the only boss using the updated Spider fruit moveset.
  • Many Blox Fruit moves have destructible physics like other attacks.
