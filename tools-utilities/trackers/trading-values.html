💡 Trading Tips & Market Insights

📈 Market Trends
Values fluctuate based on demand, updates, and seasonal events. High-demand items typically trade above their base value.
🎯 Smart Trading
Focus on items with high demand but stable values. Avoid overpaying during hype periods and look for undervalued opportunities.
⏰ Timing
Best trading times are during weekends and after updates. Avoid trading right after major nerfs or buffs until prices stabilize.
🔍 Research
Always check multiple sources and recent trades. Community discord servers often have the most up-to-date pricing information.
