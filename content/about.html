Design System & Performance

  • Unified design tokens: colors, spacing, typography, shadows, radii.
  • Light/Dark mode toggle with persistence and system preference support.
  • Sticky header/nav, chip-style breadcrumbs, responsive grids, and accessibility-first components.
  • Optimized assets with lazy loading patterns and minimal JS where possible.

Interactive Components

  • Collapsible sections with keyboard support and smooth transitions.
  • Image lightbox, tooltips, and rating systems with persistent local data.
  • Comparison modal to evaluate multiple items quickly.
  • Animated stats, counters, and progress bars for engaging visuals.

Content Coverage

  • Dedicated tools: damage calculator, fruit comparison, XP calculator, drop simulator, and more.
  • Map utilities: boss timers and interactive world map.
