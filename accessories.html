Accessories Overview

Accessories are one of the main ways that players can wear items and use them in combat or on adventures. Players can wear accessories to gain stat buffs that enhance damage or other attributes. Accessories are equipped from the inventory, and only one accessory can be worn at a time until it is removed. They are considered essential for PvP.

Talking to the Nerd will tell the player the buffs of the accessory that they are currently wearing.

Accessories are obtainable in several ways, including:

  • Purchasing an accessory from an NPC (e.g., Santa Hat)
  • Killing another player while certain requirements are met (e.g., Usopp's Hat)
  • Completing a quest (e.g., Colosseum Quest)
  • Killing a boss and getting its drop (e.g., Coat)
  • Having a certain stat level to buy said accessory (e.g., Tomoe Ring)
  • Crafting it with materials (e.g., Terror Jaw)

Stat Attributes

Each accessory can provide unique bonuses such as:

  • Offense: damage%, critical chance, element boosts
  • Defense: damage reduction, HP/energy bonuses, resistances
  • Utility: cooldown reduction, energy regen, mobility, special effects

Attributes vary by item and may synergize with specific builds.

Notes

  • No accessories currently apply debuffs or negative stats to the player.
  • Only one accessory can be worn at a time.
  • Party Hat is the only item to provide an XP boost, making it useful in early to mid-game progression or when grinding mastery.
  • Holiday Cloak, Cupid's Coat, Kitsune Mask, Kitsune Ribbon, Sanguine Cloak and Divine Cloak are the only animated accessories.
  • Dragon Mantle is the only accessory that grants more air jumps.
  • Event items may or may not be obtainable through normal means later; Seasonal Accessories may rerun yearly.
  • On console, accessories will show in the hotbar by name. Equipping them in the hotbar does nothing (visual only).
  • If the player bugs and tries to wear two accessories at once, a message appears: "Cannot hold multiple types at once."
  • While Uzoth's Cloak, Wyvern Helmet, and the Dragon Mantle are currently Admin Exclusive, this may change. D.S. Coat will not become obtainable (admin/staff-only background).
